ADJOURNED REGULAR CITY COUNCIL MEETING - 6:00 p.m.

meeting to order at 6:00 p.m. in the Council Chamber, 421 Main Street, Chico, CA.

Roll Call

Present: Huber, Morgan, Ory, Stone, Brown, Schwab
Absent: Reynolds

It was noted for the record that Item 5.3. was being continued to a future meeting so that
Councilmember Reynolds could be in attendance for the discussion.

Councilmember Ory stated he was confused about an item that was supposed to be on the agenda
that was requested by the Council to agendize the issue regarding the accounting of all funds spent
to date for the litigation of the City vs. Move the Junkyard, City vs.Ory, and Mulholland vs. the City,

Session item as it was pertaining to litigation.
